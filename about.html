Why We Are?

The essence of a brand can be found in the feelings and meaning its name evokes. So another good place to start your story is with why your business bears a certain name. Cotopaxi takes this approach by explaining the personal relevance the brand name has for its founders before continuing to tell the rest of their story through the actions they’ve taken in building the business and pursuing their cause.
